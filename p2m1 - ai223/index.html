September 30 - October 1, 2017

  • Friday: 12:00 - 6:00PM
  • Saturday: 10:00AM - 6:00PM

Ithaca, New York

The Commons

Local Produce, fresh baked goods, family entertainment, games, rides, prizes, live music and so much more!

Come and discover over 100 vendors, including local farmers, artisinal craftspeople, wineries, cideries and more, selling everything from local pumpkins to round-the-world specielties.

Check out the petting-zoo, ride the ferris-wheel, hear local musicians, enjoy giveaways, delicious food, community spirit and, of course, lots and lots of apples...

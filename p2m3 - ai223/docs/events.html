Ithaca Apple Harvest Festival has loads of activities for kids and adults of all ages. Here are just some of the things you'll be able to do:

  • Ride carnival rides and play fun carnival games on W.State Street
  • Explore the nearby fun-house and behold amazing street performances
  • Pet friendly goats and learn about how fiber is made at Laughing Goat Fiber Farm
  • Have your face painted or get a stylish henna tattoo
  • Let kids run around on the playground, centrally located on the commons, in the middle of everything

If you have kids, be sure to stop by the Family Fun Zone, courtesy of Tompkins Trust Company. Located in the central lobby of Ithaca Mall all three days of the festival, there you'll find free crafts and games while supplies last.
